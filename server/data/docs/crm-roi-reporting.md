# CRM and ROI Reporting

## Sponsor ROI

Sponsors can see ROI through booth scans, lead quality scores, meeting counts, content downloads, and session engagement. The dashboard includes top accounts, follow-up priority, scan volume by day, and conversion status.

## CRM export

Qualified leads can be exported to CSV or synced to the event CRM. Exports include attendee name, company, title, email permission status, scanned session, sponsor booth, notes, and follow-up owner.

## Analytics snapshot

The analytics snapshot summarizes registration volume, check-in rate, session attendance, feedback sentiment, PhotoApp gallery views, WhatsApp engagement, and sponsor pipeline value.
