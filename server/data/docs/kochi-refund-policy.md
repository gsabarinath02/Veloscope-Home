# Kochi Half Marathon Refund Policy

## Refund window

Refunds are available only within the published refund window. Cancellations requested on or before 15 August 2026 are eligible for a 75 percent refund. Cancellations requested from 16 August 2026 to 31 August 2026 are eligible for a 50 percent refund. No refunds are available from 1 September 2026 onward.

## Non-refundable cases

Processing fees, convenience charges, merchandise add-ons, and charity donations are non-refundable. No refund is provided for no-shows, late arrivals, missing bibs, missing timing chips, or participants who are removed for safety reasons.

## Event changes

If the organizer changes the event date due to weather, safety, government advisory, or force majeure, the registration will be moved to the new date. Refund eligibility in this case will be announced by the organizer.
