# Kochi Half Marathon Race Day Instructions

## Reporting time

Participants should reach Marine Drive Ground at least 60 minutes before their category start time. Half Marathon runners should report by 4:30 AM, Open 10K runners by 5:15 AM, Fun Run 5K runners by 6:10 AM, and Family Run 3K participants by 6:30 AM.

## Venue entry and baggage

Venue gates open at 4:00 AM. Participants must wear their bib visibly on the front of the shirt. Baggage counters are available near Gate B from 4:15 AM to 10:30 AM. Valuables should not be deposited.

## Parking and transport

Limited paid parking is available at GCDA parking near Marine Drive and at High Court Junction. Participants are encouraged to use Kochi Metro to MG Road station and the event shuttle from 4:00 AM to 5:45 AM. Two-wheeler parking is available near the service road behind Marine Drive Ground.

## Hydration and route support

Hydration stations are placed approximately every 2 kilometers. Electrolytes, water, route marshals, and restroom access are available on the route. Participants must follow marshal instructions and stay inside marked route lanes.
