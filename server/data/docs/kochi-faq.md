# Kochi Half Marathon FAQ

## Common questions

Registration is currently open and closes on 5 September 2026. The event date is Sunday, 20 September 2026. The event venue is Marine Drive Ground, Kochi. Bib collection is at Lulu Mall Convention Atrium on 18 and 19 September 2026 from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

## Race categories

The race categories are Half Marathon 21.1K, Open 10K, Fun Run 5K, and Family Run 3K. Half Marathon and Open 10K use timing chips. Fun Run 5K and Family Run 3K are non-competitive categories.

## Transfers and refunds

Registration transfer is allowed until 31 August 2026 with a fee of INR 150. Refunds are 75 percent until 15 August 2026, 50 percent from 16 August to 31 August 2026, and unavailable from 1 September 2026 onward.

## Parking and facilities

Limited paid parking is available near Marine Drive and High Court Junction. Medical support, hydration, toilets, route marshals, and baggage counters are available on race day.
