# Kochi Half Marathon Emergency and Medical Support Details

## Medical support

Medical support is available at the start and finish area and along the route. Medical aid stations are placed approximately every 2.5 kilometers. Ambulances are stationed at Marine Drive Ground, High Court Junction, and the Subhash Bose Park turn point.

## Emergency process

Participants who feel dizzy, experience chest pain, breathing difficulty, dehydration, or injury should stop running and contact the nearest route marshal or medical volunteer. The emergency control room can be reached through the helpline printed on the bib.

## Safety rules

Participants must not run with another person's bib or timing chip. Headphones should be kept at low volume so route instructions can be heard. Participants may be stopped by medical staff if continuing is unsafe.
