# Kochi Half Marathon Sponsor and Organizer Information

## Organizer

Kochi Half Marathon 2026 is organized by Veloscope Events Pvt Ltd with Eventforce as the event technology and engagement platform. Eventforce manages registration, QR check-in, bib collection workflows, WhatsApp communication, PhotoApp galleries, feedback, CRM, and ROI reporting.

## Sponsor support

Sponsors receive booth scan reports, lead export, PhotoApp gallery branding, WhatsApp engagement analytics, participant footfall estimates, and post-event ROI summaries. Sponsor reporting is available within 72 hours after the event.
