# Kochi Half Marathon Bib Collection Instructions

## Bib collection date and venue

Bib collection will take place at Lulu Mall Convention Atrium, Kochi, on Friday, 18 September 2026 and Saturday, 19 September 2026 from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Bibs will not be issued on race morning except for approved outstation emergency cases.

## Documents needed

Participants must bring the registration confirmation QR code, a government photo ID, and the registered mobile number. Accepted IDs include Aadhaar, passport, driving licence, voter ID, or college ID for students. Participants under 18 must bring guardian consent.

## Third-party bib collection

Another person can collect a bib on behalf of a participant by carrying the participant confirmation QR code, a copy of the participant ID, and an authorization message from the registered phone number.

## Bib collection rules

Participants must verify name, category, emergency contact, and T-shirt size at the counter. Once a bib is collected, the category cannot be changed.
