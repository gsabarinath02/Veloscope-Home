# Kochi Half Marathon Registration Policy

## Registration requirements

Participants must provide full name, phone number, email address, age, gender, race category, T-shirt size, emergency contact name, and emergency contact number. Participants under 18 must submit guardian consent during bib collection.

## Transfers and category changes

Registration can be transferred to another person until 31 August 2026 with a transfer fee of INR 150. Category changes are allowed until 31 August 2026 if slots are available. No transfers or category changes are permitted after 5 September 2026.

## Payment and confirmation

Registration is confirmed only after successful payment. Participants receive an email and SMS confirmation with a registration ID and QR code. The QR code is required for bib collection.

## Registration support

For registration correction requests, participants can contact support@eventforce.ai with their registration ID, registered phone number, and the requested correction.
