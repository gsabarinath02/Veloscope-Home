# Attendee Guide 2025

## Badge pickup and check-in

Badge pickup is available at all onsite registration counters. Attendees need to present a valid photo ID and either a QR code from their confirmation email or their order number. Pickup hours are May 20 from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM, May 21 from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and May 22 from 7:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Attendees can also print a badge at the self-service kiosks near the main entrance.

## Parking and venue arrival

Recommended parking is in the Javits Center public garage on 11th Avenue. The attendee drop-off point is the North Hall entrance. Accessible entry is available from the same entrance with staff assistance at the registration help desk.

## Agenda and session reminders

The agenda is available in the event app and can be filtered by track, speaker, and room. Attendees can save sessions to receive push and WhatsApp reminders 20 minutes before the session starts.
