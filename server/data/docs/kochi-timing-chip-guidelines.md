# Kochi Half Marathon Timing Chip Guidelines

## Timing chip usage

Timing chips are included for the Half Marathon 21.1K and Open 10K categories. The timing chip is attached to the bib and must not be folded, removed, pierced, or exchanged with another runner.

## Lost or damaged chip

If a participant loses the timing chip before the race, they must report to the timing help desk at least 45 minutes before start time. Replacement may be subject to availability and a fee of INR 1,000. If the chip is lost, damaged, exchanged, or not detected at timing mats, the participant may not receive an official timing certificate.

## Timing mats and certificates

Timing mats are placed at the start, split points, and finish. Participants must cross all timing mats on the official route. Digital timing certificates will be available within 48 hours after the event.
